Privacy Policy

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Hungry Club (hereinafter referred to as the service provider), operating at HungryClub.fi.

2. Contact details

Please contact us regarding registry matters by email to hello (at) hungryclub.fi.

3. Name of the register

Hungry Club service user database.

4. Purpose of processing personal data

The primary basis for processing is consent. Consent can be withdrawn at any time. The site only uses information provided by registered users. Personal data is used to provide the service, provide information about the service, maintain it, and for marketing. Data may only be disclosed to service partners for purposes that support the website's mission.

The Service contains advertisements so that we can keep the Service free to use. These may collect cookies to target interests. We also collect pseudonymized visitor statistics to track the most popular features of the service and total visitor numbers.

5. Data retention & 6. Content

We retain personal data for as long as the user uses the service. Accounts are automatically deleted if it has been more than four years since the last login. We collect:

  • Contact Info. Name and email address provided during registration.
  • Identification. Username and encrypted password (not stored in plain text).
  • Privacy Control. Users decide the level of publicity for their information; by default, only the username is secret.

8. Data Transfers & 9. Protection

Hungry Club does not disclose personal data to third parties outside the EU and EEA without separate notification. We follow general recommendations for information security to keep your data confidential.

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and local storage to recognize browsers and count unique visitors. Third parties (like measurement providers) may also place cookies to collect statistics. You can prevent or clear cookies via your browser settings at any time.

11. Rights of the Data Subject

We respond to all privacy requests within a maximum of 30 days. Your rights include:

  • Right to Inspect: Request a copy of your stored data via a signed written request.
  • Right to Correct: Demand correction of incorrect information.
  • Right to Delete: Request complete removal from all Hungry Club systems.
  • Data Portability: Request a transfer of your provided data to another system in a common format.

12. Changes & 13. Complaints

We reserve the right to change this policy as we develop our services or as legislation evolves. Users have the right to lodge a complaint with the Finnish supervisory authority at tietosuoja.fi.
